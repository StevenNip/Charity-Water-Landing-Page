Our Story

Charity: water was founded with one goal: to bring clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. Since 2006, we've funded water projects around the world, serving millions of people. We believe in radical transparency, and every dollar you give is tracked to a specific project so you can see the impact your donation has. Our journey began with a simple idea and a bold vision—and today, that vision continues to change lives.
